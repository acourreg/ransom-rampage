#### Archetype: The Neobank Disruptor

**id**: company_neobank
**sector**: Retail Banking / Challenger Bank
**description**: A "mobile-first" bank that burns VC cash to acquire users with shiny metal cards. The infrastructure is a mix of modern microservices and painful regulatory requirements.

**Typical Infrastructure Nodes**:
- **cdn_waf**: Front-line protection for the mobile API.
- **auth_service**: The "Identity Broker" managing user logins and 2FA.
- **relational_db**: The "Solid-State SQL" holding the ledger of truth.
- **kyc_aml**: The "Identity Scan" for onboarding the unbanked.
- **notification_service**: The "Push Pigeon" for transaction alerts.
- **payment_engine**: The "Pay-Flow" core for managing transfers.
- **admin_dashboard**: The "God-Mode" panel for support agents.
- **monitoring_logging**: The "Log-Observe-Eye" for the 4 AM crashes.

**Typical Edge Patterns**:
- High-frequency bursts from **api_gateway** to **auth_service**.
- Heavy bidirectional sync between **relational_db** and **payment_engine**.
- Async events from **payment_engine** to **notification_service** via **message_broker**.

**Critical Nodes**: 
- **relational_db**: If this corrupts, the bank's balance sheet is gone.
- **auth_service**: If down, 100% of users are locked out.

#### Archetype: The Global Payment Rail (PSP)

**id**: company_psp
**sector**: Payment Service Provider / Merchant Acquiring
**description**: The middleman of the internet. You take a 2.9% + 30c cut of everyone's dreams. Scale is the only thing that matters, and a 1s delay in processing is a million-dollar loss.

**Typical Infrastructure Nodes**:
- **load_balancer**: Handling millions of concurrent checkout requests.
- **payment_engine**: The high-performance "PCI Processor."
- **fraud_detection**: The "ML-Pattern-Matcher" killing card-testers.
- **card_network**: The "External Rail" connecting to Visa/MC.
- **reconciliation_engine**: The "Penny-Match" bot for merchant payouts.
- **secrets_manager**: The "Vault" for merchant API keys.
- **nosql_db**: Storing petabytes of non-critical transaction metadata.
- **service_mesh**: Managing the chaos of 200+ micro-internal services.

**Typical Edge Patterns**:
- High-speed unidirectional flow from **api_gateway** to **fraud_detection**.
- Strict, low-latency loop between **payment_engine** and **card_network**.
- Massive batch exports from **nosql_db** to **data_warehouse**.

**Critical Nodes**:
- **payment_engine**: The literal revenue pipe.
- **card_network**: External dependency that can kill your SLA.

#### Archetype: The HFT Alpha-Seeker

**id**: company_hft
**sector**: High-Frequency Trading / Prop Shop
**description**: You don't have customers; you have algorithms. You compete on nanoseconds. Your infra is essentially a supercomputer trying to front-run the rest of the world.

**Typical Infrastructure Nodes**:
- **trading_engine**: The "Nano-Trade-Matcher" execution core.
- **message_broker**: The "Kafkaesque Bus" for market data feeds.
- **risk_scoring**: The "Monte-Carlo" pre-trade check.
- **blockchain_ledger**: The "Immutable Ledger" for post-trade settlement.
- **data_warehouse**: The "OLAP Lakehouse" for backtesting strategies.
- **secrets_manager**: Protecting the private keys of the trading bots.
- **monitoring_logging**: Ultra-low-overhead tracing for "Gripe" detection.

**Typical Edge Patterns**:
- Direct Memory Access (DMA) bypasses from **message_broker** to **trading_engine**.
- Pre-execution check: **trading_engine** ↔ **risk_scoring** (must be < 1ms).

**Critical Nodes**:
- **trading_engine**: If this lags, you are trading on "Old News" (Bankrupt).
- **risk_scoring**: If it breaks, the bots might go "Rogue" and sell the company's own building.

**id**: company_crypto_vault
**sector**: Web3 Infrastructure / Digital Assets
**description**: You hold the keys to the kingdom. Your whole business model is "We haven't been hacked yet." A mix of cold storage and high-availability hot wallets.

**Typical Infrastructure Nodes**:
- **secrets_manager**: The "Vault" for multi-sig private keys.
- **blockchain_ledger**: The "Blocky-Chain" for on-chain verification.
- **auth_service**: Heavy-duty IAM with hardware token support.
- **relational_db**: Off-chain ledger for internal user balances.
- **kyc_aml**: Sanctions scanning to avoid the FBI’s "Most Wanted" list.
- **api_gateway**: Exposing institutional-grade APIs.
- **cloud_kyc**: For the biometric "Selfie Checks."

**Typical Edge Patterns**:
- Air-gapped manual triggers from **admin_dashboard** to **secrets_manager**.
- Constant polling from **blockchain_ledger** to **relational_db** for deposit detection.

**Critical Nodes**:
- **secrets_manager**: If the keys are lost or stolen, it's "Game Over" forever.
- **blockchain_ledger**: If out of sync, you are literally flying blind.
