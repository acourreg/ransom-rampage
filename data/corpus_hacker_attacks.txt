#### Phishing-Pizza-Delivery

**id**: attack_phishing
**category**: compromise
**target_node_types**: human
**ap_cost**: 1

**description**: Stacy from Accounting received an email promising a "Free Friday Pizza Voucher." She clicked. Now Byte has her credentials. Exploits the fact that your employees are the squishiest part of your infrastructure.

**mechanics**:
- Success if target.defense < 6.
- Sets node.compromised = true.
- If node.has_mfa is false, defense is treated as 2.

**impact**:
- Entry point for lateral movement.
- Reputation: -0.05 if leaked.
- Visibility: Moderate (hard to distinguish from legitimate login).

#### The-3AM-Encryption-Routine

**id**: attack_ransomware
**category**: encryption
**target_node_types**: database, server
**ap_cost**: 2

**description**: The dreaded .crypt extension appears on all files. Byte has locked your data and left a note in Comic Sans. Your revenue flows are now a flat line.

**prerequisites**: Target node must be compromised.

**mechanics**:
- Sets node.locked = true.
- Disables all Revenue Flows passing through this node.
- Triggers regulator.breach_timer = 3.

**impact**:
- Revenue: -100% for affected flows.
- Cash: Huge recovery cost or ransom payment.
- Reputation: Heavy drop per turn locked.

#### Smoke-Screen-DDoS
